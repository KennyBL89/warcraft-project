About Warcraft

Explore Azeroth

Azeroth is the name of the world in which the majority of the Warcraft series is set. At its core dwells a slumbering world-soul, the nascent spirit of a titan.

Long ago, it was invaded by the Old Gods, eldritch abominations from the Void. When the Pantheon arrived, the titans imprisoned the Old Gods deep beneath the earth.

Warcraft factions

Horde

The Horde is made up of orcs, forsaken, tauren, trolls, blood elves, and most recently, goblins.

Alliance

Humans, night elves, dwarves, gnomes, draenei, and the savage worgen make up the illustrious Alliance.

Pandaren

The enigmatic pandaren are one of the most elusive races of Azeroth. Hailing from Pandaria and the Wandering Isle.
